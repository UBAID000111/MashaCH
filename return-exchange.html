Return & Exchange Policy

Easy Returns • Hassle-Free Exchanges • Customer Satisfaction

Your Satisfaction Matters

At Masha Wear, every outfit is carefully packed before dispatch. If you receive a damaged product or require a size exchange, our support team is here to assist you quickly.

Return Policy

  • Return request must be raised within 3 Days (72 Hours).
  • Unboxing video is mandatory.
  • Product must be unused and unwashed.
  • Original tags & packaging required.
  • Applicable for damaged, defective or incorrect products.
  • Refund processed after inspection.

Exchange Policy

  • Exchange request within 3 Days (72 Hours).
  • Only size exchanges are accepted.
  • One exchange allowed per order.
  • Subject to stock availability.
  • If size unavailable, store coupon of equal value (valid for 90 days).

Not Eligible

✔ Used Products
✔ Washed Garments
✔ Missing Tags
✔ Damaged By Customer
✔ Requests After 72 Hours
✔ On Sale Products
